**What you will need:**
*   100 ml Effective Microorganisms (EM)
*   100 ml natural vinegar
*   100 ml wine or brandy
*   100 g jaggery
*   600 ml water
*   Garlic and chili paste (optional, to make it stronger)

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1.  **Mix the ingredients:** In a container, mix the 100 ml EM, 100 ml vinegar, and 100 ml wine or brandy.
2.  **Add the fermenter:** Add 100 g of jaggery and 600 ml of water to the mixture.

5.  **Dilute and Spray:** After 7 days, dilute the fermented liquid with water at a ratio of 1 part spray to 1000 parts water.
